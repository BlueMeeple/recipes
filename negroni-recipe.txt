Ingredients:
 - 1 ounce gin
 - 1 ounce Campari
 - 1 ounce sweet (red) vermouth
 - orange

Directions:
 1. Fill shaker with ice cubes.
 2. Add gin, Campari, and vermouth.  Shake.
 3. Strain into tumbler.
 4. Add ice to tumbler.  (Optional)
 5. Shave a 3" x 1" strip of peel off of the orange.  Rub around rim and drop into tumbler.
